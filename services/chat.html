AI Character Engine Alpha

Welcome to the alpha release of our AI Character Engine Demo for "Melodies of the Heart"! Immerse yourself in a sneak peek of the interactive character experience that awaits in our upcoming game.


What To Expect:


Meet the characters

Engage in dynamic conversations with two characters from "Melodies of the Heart" — Kajiwara Kame and Kishi Shizuka, and a supporting Vocaloid character — Hatsune Miku. Each character has a dumbed down prompt, preserving the essence of their personalities without revealing spoilers.


Converse with emotion

Chat freely with Kame and Shizuka by simply addressing them. Witness their emotions unfold in real-time as they respond to your words, showcasing a glimpse of the emotional depth our AI Character Engine, powered by ChatGPT, brings to interactive storytelling.


API coming soon!

This demo is a preview of the upcoming API release. Experience the seamless integration of our Character Engine and get a taste of the possibilities for your own projects.


Disclaimer

Please note that this demo is in its alpha release stage. While we strive for a smooth experience, you may encounter it sometimes having an identity crisis, straight up refusing to answer, and other unexpected behavior. Your valuable feedback will aid us in refining and enhancing the engine.


Conversations are NOT private

As this is a demo, and is on an alpha stage, we'd like to gain as much insight as possible when it comes to troubleshooting problems. In this case, your conversation with Kame, Shizuka, or Miku wouldn't be private, so please refrain from giving out personal information.
